Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 30 days before the trip. Cancellations made 15-29 days before receive a 50% refund. No refunds for cancellations made less than 15 days before the trip.

How much deposit is required?

A 20% deposit is required to secure your booking. The remaining balance is due 30 days before your trip start date.

Can I customize my itinerary?

Yes, we offer customization options for all our packages. Please mention your preferences in the Special Requests field or contact our travel consultants directly.

What documents do I need?

You'll need a valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond your travel dates, and an Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) or visa for Sri Lanka. We can assist with the visa application process if needed.

Is travel insurance mandatory?

While not mandatory, we strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect your trip investment against unforeseen circumstances. We offer comprehensive travel insurance packages that cover trip cancellation, medical emergencies, and more.
